Beagle

Weight: 10-11kg
Coat Short haired, hard coat of medium length
Color Many colors

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound. The Beagle is a scent hound, develop primarily for hunting
hare (beagling). With a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct, the
beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and
foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent but single-
minded. It is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited
health problems.
